Welcome to my homepage for kung fu!

Dedicated to the promotion of kung fu for health and wellbeing.

I have found kung fu to be a precious treasure in my life. It has provided me with both physical strength and health, as well as formidable mental resilience, confidence, and will. This website is my celebration and "homage" to the Chinese art of kung fu, my particular style (Lung Shou Pai), my Grandmaster Sigung Norman Pedelahore, and all masters and practitioners of kung fu.
